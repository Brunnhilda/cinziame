Design of a catalogue of scientific travels organised by a German NGO


The Problem

Wildlife Explorers had just launched and had to raise awareness and promote its activities to the public.


The Solution

The brochure had to show the variety and beauty of destinations proposed while underlining the scientific aspect of the travels, as all the guides are science PhDs.

I imagined the concept of travels as scientific adventures and designed the catalogue accordingly.










My role

Art direction, graphic design

Tools

Photoshop, InDesign
