Get to know me

What I did, where I worked

As a designer I worked with cross-functional teams (art directors, webdesigners, motion designers, developers, project managers) and handled multiple projects on different media from conception to execution (branding, visuals, infographics, landing pages, social media campaigns, newsletters, leaflets, animations and videos).


I lived in Italy, France and the US.


In Italy I learned limitless creativity and out-of-the-box thinking, in France I became mindful of details and managed to convey effortless looking and clean designs and in the US I learned to innovate in a framework defined by data and results.
My passion for classical music brought me to found Classicagenda: a digital magazine of which I'm editor in chief and manager of a team on 15 editors.
