Redesign of the newsletter of an online supermarket


The Problem

AuchanDirect sends every week a newsletter to its subscribers that are segmented geographically. The newsletter needed a restyling in order to be more attractive and fresh. My design scope was limited because the content was managed via a CMS and there was no budget for reprogramming it.




The Solution

I imagined a modular system based on two types of components, the ones that could be partially or fully customized and to create a template for the client (the online marketing department)




Wireframing

I designed a global wireframe displaying all the modules, organised by content and priority.


User Interface

The UI is simple and clean, the color codes I imagined help to distinguish the different kinds of promotions




My role

Information Architecture, User App Flow, Wireframes, UX/UI design

Tools

Marvel, Figma
