A showcase of the logos I designed



Classicagenda

Classical music magazine

After a study of the logos of the main stakeholders in the classical music world, I came up whith a round pictogram with a golden background.

Inside there's a quarter rest, a musical symbol indicating an interval of silence, a pause. Classicagenda is a place where people can enjoy a moment of leisure. The logotype has an elegant font, with the first part in italic to empahasise the classical music element.



Campagna Adda

Environmental campaign

Campagna Adda is a campaign for the valorisation of the Adda river region by the NGO Legambiente.

The design of the logo was challenging because it had to represent four areas, so I chose to include more icons, water symbolizes the river, but also the dams and the rain, the fish represents the aquatic creatures and the local's passion for fishing, the tree indicates the vegetation of the Adda Natural Park and finally the house symbolizes the remaining farmhouses and the human activity, in harmony with nature. All this within a symbol that recalls the soft course of the river.



Chances for nature

Environmental NGO

The logo had to highlight the care given to earth's flora and fauna. I drew a lemur, which is a threatened species and a plant shoot, symbolizing growth and rebirth. The rounded form suggest the earth but also the idea of a virtuous circle.



Two2Tango

App to find dance partners

I chose dark red and black, the colors associated with tango. The name Two2Tango plays on the pronunciation of "two" and "to" which I decided to stress in the logo. Starting from the acronym T2T I designed the pictogram, then I isolated the shape of the 2 and used it in the logotype.



Teadesign

Graphic design agency

The pictogram is the acronym of TeaDesign and the shape of a cup of tea.



Fuori di casa

Cultural events platform

Fuoridicasa (Out-of-home) is a project that I conceived that received a grant by the Province of Lecco. The logo represents an arrow directed towards the outside, with a laptop, while tilted by 90°, it becomes a house with a wide-open door.



Echeia

Cultural non-profit

Echeia is a young professionals and academics association interested in art and architectural history. The logo is based on a drawing given me by the client, showing a mythical creature.



Bounty sub

Diving association

I wanted to represent water and diving in an original and fresh way, so I designed a stylised seaweed and a few bubbles to evoke an underwater scenery and tank air.



La fabbrica delle idee

Graphic design agency

Logo for a cultural NGO. I designed an ironic and witty logo with a cool colour symbolising the factory, the production site and a warm colour coming out from the chimney, because the NGO produces creativity.



Lab

Creative hub

The hub is a place where creative professionals network and organize events.
