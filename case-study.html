The journey of en entrepreneur

Building a startup from scratch

  • October 2012

    From a passion to a business idea

    As my father was a musician I grew up listening to classical music. I spend my evenings attending operas and concerts but I was frustrated because it was difficult to know what was going on and afraid of missing events. That's why I decided one day to combine this passion with my professional skills as a designer and create Classicagenda.

  • November 2012

    Doing marketing research

    I spent four months researching information about the classical music community, the competitors and the possible business model. I also attended a workshop organised by the Chamber of Commerce in order to learn how to build a company.

  • April 2013

    Preparing a business plan

    I also attended a workshop organised by the Chamber of Commerce in order to learn how to build a company and built a business plan analysing the data I collected.

  • June 2013

    Finding a team

    In order to build the website I needed developers so I contacted a web agency I had collaborated with. The founders liekd the project and became my business partners.

  • July 2013

    Designing the product

    Time for UX design! I created personas, defined the user flow, organised the content and built a sitemap. designing marketing campaigns, animating and coding, queuing for baguettes.

  • September 2013

    Prototyping & Testing

    I designed a prototype of the website and made user tests. One validated, I delivered the elements to the developers to start thinking about the database organisation.

  • November 2013

    Creating a visual identity

    I created Classicagenda's corporate identity, in order to apply it to the website.

  • December 2013

    Product design

    According to the prototypes I created the website look and feel.
    Discover how I built Classicagenda's identity

  • January 2014

    Supervising the development

    During the sprints of the development I checked if everything was conform to my design. Some compromises had to be made in order to achieve the deadline

  • Mai 2014

    Launching the website

    Classicagenda was ready to go!
    Take a look at the website

  • 2014-2017

    Managing the company and the team

    Working alone is not easy, but I had the chance to meet other classical music lovers who wanted to join the adventure. Classicagenda has today a team of 15 people that I coordinate.

  • 2018

    Ensuring growth

    I manage the marketing & brand strategy and the product development.
    Visit Classicagenda
